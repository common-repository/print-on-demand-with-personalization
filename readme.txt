== Description ==
Are you tired of dealing with overseas print on demand companies and the long ship times? PrintTech/IconEcom is USA based and products are generally turned around within 3-5 days and ship time is 5 days or less to anywhere within the USA. International shipping is also available.

In business for over 30 years, PrintTech/IconEcom sources products and does screen printing, direct to garment printing, embroidery, full dye sublimation, inventory management along with pick, pack and fulfillment anywhere in the world!

Allow our 165,000 Sq. / ft. facility in Missouri to supply all your printing needs on state of the art printing equipment.

#### Simple Steps for Success

1.  Upload your design to PrintTech/IconEcom
2.  We print for you
3.  We pack for you
4.  We ship for you
5.  You profit

Your product cost is listed in your PrintTech/IconEcom account. You mark up the products however much you want to determine your profit.
